Web
Web is a mod that expands on spiders by giving them new behaviors and animations. Spiders can now shoot webs to trap players, and baby spiders will hatch from eggs found in Spider Egg Clusters underground. Their attacks and style are inspired by the way Minecraft Dungeons implemented spiders
Universe 76
Universe 76 is the aftermath of a deleted Minecraft world. It shows what happened to that world, corruption. Corruption spread across the world, infecting biomes and mobs and sending them to a new dimension, the Corrupted Nexus.

About PotatoWolfie

I, PotatoWolfie, am a Minecraft creator and mod developer who enjoys making vanilla friendly mods, but I like to experiment with some mods that go beyond what vanilla would add. I work as hard as I can to make quality mods (disregarding Universe 76) that aren't unbearable to play. I work with creators like Demidiant, who I have led projects with in the past. Latest Project

The latest mod released, Web, expands the role of spiders in Minecraft. With new behaviors and fresh animations, spiders are more dynamic than ever. They can shoot webs to trap players, and Spider Egg Clusters found underground may burst open with baby spiders. Inspired by Minecraft Dungeons, this mod makes every encounter with spiders more tense and dangerous.
