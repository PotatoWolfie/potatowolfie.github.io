Webs
Spiders, when fighting, can shoot webs at you. When these webs land on you, they prevent you from moving for two seconds, before releasing you. In this time, all spiders in the area come and feast on you. This applies to any other creature that happens to get in a web.
Spiderlings
Baby spiders, also known as spiderlings, are the children of spiders, of course. They come from Spider Eggs, found in caves. Usually generating in clusters. They act how the vanilla spiders would work, they "learn" how to shoot webs when they become an adult.

about the Mod

Web is a mod that expands on spiders by giving them new behaviors and animations. Spiders can now shoot webs to trap players, and baby spiders will hatch from eggs found in Spider Egg Clusters underground. Their attacks and style are inspired by the way Minecraft Dungeons implemented spiders

In addition to their new mechanics, spiders can be found near Spider Eggs in caves . These eggs will eventually break on their own, releasing spiderlings that are smaller and faster, but have less health.

Featured article

The Spider Web is a block that can be used as an item. When placed on the ground, you can create a spider web similar to what a spider shoots at you. Except this one has cobweb physics. If you hang the spider web from a ceiling, you can get hanging webs, which you can climb. If you throw the spider web, you can throw it exactly how a spider shoots one. You can shoot it at mobs or players to get them stuck for two seconds. Spiders in the nearby area will also take note and attack.
