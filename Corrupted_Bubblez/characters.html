Characters

Bubblez

Bubblez is a humor-motivated figure who generally has no idea what he is doing; however, he adapts to whatever situation he finds himself in. He is curious, and he will notice small irregularities when things don't appear right. Despite his light-hearted attitude, Bubblez is able to take things seriously if the situation calls for it. He prefers improvisation rather than having a plan, so he becomes unpredictable to both himself and his enemies.

Bubblez with fish wings. He has a crown atop his head.

CW

CW, Bubblez's deal buddy, is a highly controlling figure. CW prefers having control over any situation he finds himself in, and he will find any way to get that control. He follows his first instinct in situations, but he poorly adapts to situations if they don't go the way he planned it. He likes to wear on a grin on his face quite often. He's rational, but he's overly critical. When CW makes a minor mistake, he is more likely to make that same mistake again.

CW leaning against a wall.

Lb

Lb, pronounced "Pound," is Bubblez's pet cat. He gets revealed during the first trailer of Corrupted Bubblez, and he is a major reason why Bubblez broke his deal with CW. Lb served as a good distraction and helper for Bubblez in the lab. Whether it be emotional support or flicking a lever across the room, Lb has proven time and time again to be a loyal companion for Bubblez.

Lb on Bubblez's shoulders. Bubblez is giving him a fish.

Bubbloids

Bubbloids are seemingly living bubbles that roam the lab Bubblez worked in. They come in three sizes: small, medium, and large. The smaller they are, the faster they are, but the less durable they are. The bigger ones are slower but more durable. They help Bubblez around the lab as needed, but they don't do any major activities in the lab.

Four Bubbloids gathered near each other. All three sizes are shown: small, medium, and large.
