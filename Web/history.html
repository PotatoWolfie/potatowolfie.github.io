Mod Development History

Concept for Web

The idea for Web started with one of my friends, Demidiant. He had recreated the vanilla Spider texture, and even made a couple custom ones. This reminded me of Minecraft Dungeons and how spiders work in that game. Then I had the idea to implement this and thus this mod had been conceived.

Version 1.0.0

The first major release introduced spider-themed blocks, new Spider behaviors, Baby spiders, and new web mechanics that established the identity of Web.
