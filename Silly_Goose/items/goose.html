Goose Meat

Goose is a food item obtained from geese. It can be cooked in a furnace, smoker, or campfire to make Cooked Goose.

Obtaining

Mob Loot

Adult geese drop 1-2 raw goose when killed. The amount is affected by the Looting enchantment.

Item Default Looting I Looting II Looting III
Icon Name Quantity Average Quantity Average Quantity Average Quantity Average
Goose 1 1.00 1-2 1.50 1-3 2.00 1-4 2.50
Cooking

Goose can be cooked into Cooked Goose using various methods. All cooking methods grant 0.35 experience upon completion.

Usage

Food

Both raw and cooked goose can be eaten by the player to restore hunger and saturation. Cooked goose is significantly more nutritious than raw goose.

Goose (Raw)
3 hunger
1.2 saturation
Cooked Goose
7 hunger
5.6 saturation

Cooking Methods

Raw goose can be cooked using any heat source that processes food items. Each method has different cooking times but provides the same result.

Method Cooking Time Experience
Furnace 10 seconds 0.35 XP
Smoker 5 seconds 0.35 XP
Campfire 30 seconds 0.35 XP
