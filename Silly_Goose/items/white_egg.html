White Egg

White Eggs are throwable projectile items that can hatch into baby geese when thrown. There are three variants of white eggs, each associated with a different goose variant and found in different biomes.

Obtaining

Egg Laying

White eggs are laid by adult geese every 5-10 minutes (6000-12000 ticks). The type of egg laid depends on the goose's variant:

  • Temperate Geese: Lay White Eggs
  • Cold Geese: Lay Big White Eggs
  • Warm Geese: Lay Small White Eggs
Natural Sources

White eggs can be collected from the ground after adult geese lay them. They appear as item entities and can be picked up like any other item.

Usage

As a Projectile

All three types of white eggs can be thrown by "using" the item. When thrown, they travel through the air similar to regular chicken eggs and snowballs. Upon impact with an entity or block, the egg has a chance to hatch.

Hatching

Hatch Probability

When a thrown white egg collides with something, there is a 12.5% chance (1 in 8) that it will hatch. If the egg does hatch, there is an additional 3.125% chance (1 in 32) that 4 baby geese will spawn instead of just 1.
