Goose

Geese are semi-aquatic neutral mobs that prefer staying near water. They exhibit sophisticated territorial behaviors, can hold grudges against players, and may even pick up swords to use as weapons in hit-and-run attacks.

Spawning

Natural Spawning

Geese naturally spawn in areas near water bodies. They require at least 6 water blocks within a 12-block horizontal radius and 5-block vertical range to spawn. Geese can spawn on blocks with ice covering water underneath.

Variants

There are three goose variants that spawn based on biome:

  • Temperate Goose: The standard variant found in temperate biomes
  • Cold Goose: Found in cold and snowy biomes, lays large white eggs
  • Warm Goose: Found in warm biomes, lays small white eggs
Family Groups

Adult geese have a 30% chance to spawn with 1-3 baby geese nearby during natural spawning, creating small family groups.

Behavior

Water Preference

Each goose has a randomly assigned preference for either water or land. This preference changes every 4-7 minutes (4800-7200 ticks). When geese spend 30-60 seconds in their non-preferred environment, they actively seek to return to their preferred habitat.

Egg Laying

Adult geese lay eggs every 5-10 minutes (6000-12000 ticks). The egg type depends on the goose variant:

  • Temperate: White Egg
  • Cold: Big White Egg
  • Warm: Small White Egg
Weapon Pickup

Adult geese can pick up swords from the ground to use as weapons. Players can trade any type of seeds to convince a goose to drop its weapon, and the goose won't pick up another sword for 20 seconds after trading.

Combat

Hit-and-Run Attacks

Geese employ a unique hit-and-run combat strategy when attacking players:

  • Approach the player at 1.5× normal speed
  • Strike once with their beak or equipped sword
  • Immediately flee 8-16 blocks away
  • Wait 5-7 minutes (6000-8400 ticks) before considering another attack
  • Armed geese are prioritized as attackers over unarmed ones
Death Messages

Geese use three different death messages randomly:

  • Goose Bother: "was bothered to death"
  • Goose Peck: "was pecked to death"
  • Goose Honk: "was honked at by Goose"
Revenge

Geese have a revenge mechanic:

  • When a goose is killed by a player, all geese within 64 blocks remember that player
  • Geese will remember this player for 2-3 1/3 hours
  • Geese have a countdown of 60-120 seconds between attack attempts
  • During revenge attacks, geese strike 2-3 times before fleeing
